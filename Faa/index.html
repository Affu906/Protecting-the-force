6. SAFE DEVICE PRACTICES
PROTECTING
THE FORCE “Cyber Aware Police,
Cyber Safe Citizens”

DO'S

  • Use strong unique passwords
  • Enable multi-factor authentication
  • Verify sender details
  • Lock devices when not in use
  • Update software regularly
  • Report suspicious activities to 1930

DON'TS

  • Never share OTPs or passwords
  • Don't share case info on social media
  • Don't plug unknown USB drives
  • Avoid public Wi-Fi for official work
  • Don't click suspicious links
  • Never download unknown attachments
